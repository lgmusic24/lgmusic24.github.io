Psycological Thrillers

By Luisa Gonzalez

The Perry

The Perry is a psychological thriller about man who sees nightmares of a mysterious man man being burned alive in his own apartment room. While his friends don't believe him, he becomes frustrated and does some research the apartment. The Perry was once a hotel that was burnt down over 80 years ago. Every single person died that day. Including those that weren't found.
